Achievements

  • He recovered the unity of the party and the trust of former PSOE voters
  • Ha abogado por la reforma de la Constitución, proponiendo un modelo federal para España,59​ así como volver a cambiar su artículo 135 o reforzar el laicismo del sistema educativo.
  • He promoted the reform of the Historical Memory Law to grant rights to those who suffered persecution or violence during the civil war and the dictatorship.
  • His government took the measure of exhuming the remains of dicator Franco from the Valley of the Fallen
  • The Sanchez government offered the port of Valencia to disembark the Aquarius ship, with 629 immigrants on board, after Italy and Malta denied entry into their ports, thus avoiding a humanitarian catastrophe.
  • He approved the decree that set the minimum wage at 900 euros, the largest increase since 1977.
  • His Government cut the so-called "fallen from the sky profits" of the electricity companies, to mitigate the rise in the price of the electricity bill, caused by the historic rise in gas prices throughout Europe
  • He promoted the Work Reform of 2022 in order to reduce temporary employment and the unemployment rate
  • To address the effects of the war in Ukraine and inflation on the domestic economy, the Government announced in December 2022 the reduction of VAT on fresh or essential foods.
  • The Pedro Sánchez government imposed a temporary special tax on electricity, gas and oil companies. También otro a los bancos
  • The Government launched the so-called "cultural youth bonus", an aid of 400 euros so that 18-year-olds can spend in the cultural sector
