About

First Years

Pedro Sanchez was born in Madrid in 1972. His father was an economist, his mother a lawyer and his brother is an orchestra director. He started studying Economics in the Complutense University in Madrid in 1990, graduating in 1995. He joined the Socialist Spanish Party (PSOE) in 1993.

Beginnings in Politics

In 2004, he became a council member in the municipality of Madrid. Between 2004 and 2009 he was one of the 320 members of the General Assembly of the Bank Caja Madrid. From 2008 to 2013 he was a professor in Economic Structure and History of Economic Thought in the University Camilo Jose Cela.

Member of the Congress of Deputies

He became a member of the Congress in September, 2009. He run for President of the Community of Madrid in 2011, though he lost. He worked in the OCU and achieved his PhD in Economics in 2012. In January, 2013, he became a member of the Congress of Deputies again.
